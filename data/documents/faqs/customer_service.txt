## Common Questions and Solutions

### Account Access Issues

**Forgot Password:**
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter username and email
4. Follow reset instructions sent to email
5. Create new strong password

**Locked Account:**
After 3 failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for security.
- Wait 30 minutes and try again, or
- Call customer service for immediate unlock

### Card Issues

**Lost or Stolen Card:**
1. Call 1-800-555-BANK immediately
2. Report card as lost/stolen
3. Review recent transactions for fraud
4. Order replacement card (arrives in 5-7 business days)
5. Request expedited shipping if needed ($25 fee)

**Card Declined:**
Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Daily limit reached
- Card expired
- Security hold (traveling?)
- Incorrect PIN attempts

**Solution:** Call customer service or check mobile app for specific reason

### Fraud Protection

**Fraud Alerts:**
We monitor your account 24/7 for suspicious activity.

**If We Detect Fraud:**
- You'll receive text, email, or phone call
- Verify whether transactions are yours
- Card may be temporarily blocked
- Fraudulent charges will be removed
- New card issued if needed

**Zero Liability:**
You're not responsible for unauthorized transactions reported promptly.

**Report Fraud Immediately:**
- Call 1-800-555-BANK
- Or use mobile app's "Report Fraud" feature

### Disputes and Complaints

**Dispute a Transaction:**
1. Gather transaction details
2. Contact merchant first (may resolve faster)
3. If unresolved, file dispute with us:
   - Online: www.bank.com/disputes
   - Phone: 1-800-555-BANK
   - Mail: Disputes Department, PO Box 12345
4. Investigation takes 30-45 days
5. Provisional credit may be issued within 10 days

**File a Complaint:**
We take complaints seriously. Contact:
- Branch manager
- Customer service supervisor
- Email: complaints@communitybank.com
- Mail: Customer Relations, PO Box 12345

**Escalation:**
If not satisfied with resolution:
- Ask for case to be escalated
- Request corporate customer relations
- File complaint with regulatory agencies
