# Savings Account Policies

## Account Types

### Basic Savings Account
Our Basic Savings Account is perfect for those just starting their savings journey.

**Features:**
- Minimum opening deposit: $25
- No monthly maintenance fee with $300 minimum balance
- Interest rate: 0.50% APY
- Unlimited deposits
- Up to 6 withdrawals per month
- FDIC insured up to $250,000

**Fees:**
- Monthly maintenance fee: $5 (waived with $300 minimum balance)
- Excessive withdrawal fee: $10 per transaction over 6 per month
- Paper statement fee: $2 per month (waived with e-statements)

### Premium Savings Account
Higher interest rates for larger balances.

**Features:**
- Minimum opening deposit: $1,000
- No monthly maintenance fee with $5,000 minimum balance
- Tiered interest rates:
  - $1,000 - $9,999: 3.50% APY
  - $10,000 - $24,999: 4.00% APY
  - $25,000+: 4.50% APY
- Unlimited deposits and withdrawals
- Free checks
- Priority customer service
- FDIC insured up to $250,000

**Fees:**
- Monthly maintenance fee: $15 (waived with $5,000 minimum balance)
- Non-sufficient funds (NSF): $35 per item

## Opening an Account

### Required Documents
To open a savings account, you'll need:
1. Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
2. Social Security Number or Tax Identification Number
3. Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement, or bank statement dated within 60 days)
4. Initial deposit (minimum varies by account type)

### Online Account Opening
You can open an account online in minutes:
1. Visit our website and click "Open Account"
2. Choose your account type
3. Enter your personal information
4. Upload required documents
5. Fund your account via electronic transfer or check deposit
6. Account is typically approved within 1 business day

### In-Branch Account Opening
Visit any of our branch locations during business hours:
- Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

## Interest and Compounding

Interest is calculated daily and compounded monthly. Interest is credited to your account on the last business day of each month.

**Annual Percentage Yield (APY)** is accurate as of January 1, 2024, and may change at any time without notice.

## Withdrawal Limits

Federal regulations limit certain types of withdrawals and transfers from savings accounts to 6 per month. Transactions that count toward this limit include:
- Online transfers
- Telephone transfers
- Automatic transfers
- Checks (if applicable)
- Debit card purchases (if applicable)

Unlimited transactions:
- ATM withdrawals
- In-person withdrawals at branches
- Deposits of any kind

## Account Closure

You may close your account at any time. To close:
1. Visit a branch or call customer service
2. Provide account verification
3. Withdraw or transfer remaining balance
4. Destroy any checks or debit cards

**Note:** Accounts with a zero balance for 90 consecutive days may be closed automatically.
