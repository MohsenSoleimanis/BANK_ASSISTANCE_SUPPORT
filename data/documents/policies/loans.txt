# Loan Products and Policies

## Personal Loans

### Unsecured Personal Loans
Borrow for any purpose without collateral.

**Loan Details:**
- Loan amounts: $1,000 - $50,000
- Terms: 12, 24, 36, 48, or 60 months
- Interest rates: 6.99% - 24.99% APR (based on creditworthiness)
- Fixed monthly payments
- No prepayment penalties
- Funds available within 1 business day

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Minimum age: 18 years old
- Minimum credit score: 620
- Minimum annual income: $25,000
- US citizen or permanent resident
- Valid bank account
- Debt-to-income ratio below 45%

**Required Documents:**
- Government-issued ID
- Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
- Proof of address
- Employment verification

### Secured Personal Loans
Lower rates with collateral.

**Loan Details:**
- Loan amounts: $5,000 - $100,000
- Terms: 12 - 84 months
- Interest rates: 4.99% - 12.99% APR
- Collateral: Vehicle, savings account, or other approved assets
- Loan-to-value ratio up to 90%

## Auto Loans

### New Car Loans
Finance your new vehicle purchase.

**Loan Details:**
- Loan amounts: Up to $100,000
- Terms: 24, 36, 48, 60, or 72 months
- Interest rates: Starting at 3.99% APR
- Finance up to 120% of vehicle value (including taxes, fees, and warranties)
- Gap insurance available

**Rate Factors:**
- Credit score
- Down payment amount
- Loan term
- Vehicle age and type

### Used Car Loans
Great rates on pre-owned vehicles.

**Loan Details:**
- Vehicles up to 7 years old
- Terms: 24 - 60 months
- Interest rates: Starting at 4.99% APR
- Maximum loan-to-value: 110%

## Mortgage Products

### Fixed-Rate Mortgages
Consistent payments throughout your loan term.

**Available Terms:**
- 15-year fixed: Starting at 5.75% APR
- 20-year fixed: Starting at 6.00% APR
- 30-year fixed: Starting at 6.25% APR

**Features:**
- Down payment as low as 3%
- PMI removal at 20% equity
- Rate lock for 60 days
- No prepayment penalties

### Adjustable-Rate Mortgages (ARM)
Lower initial rates with adjustment periods.

**Available Options:**
- 5/1 ARM: Starting at 5.25% APR
- 7/1 ARM: Starting at 5.50% APR
- 10/1 ARM: Starting at 5.75% APR

**Features:**
- Lower initial payment
- Rate caps for protection
- Conversion option to fixed rate

### Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)
Borrow against your home equity.

**Line Details:**
- Credit lines: $10,000 - $500,000
- Interest rate: Prime + 0.5% (currently 8.50% APR)
- Draw period: 10 years
- Repayment period: 20 years
- Interest-only payments during draw period
- Maximum combined loan-to-value: 90%

## Credit Cards

### Cashback Rewards Card
Earn cash back on every purchase.

**Card Features:**
- No annual fee
- 2% cash back on all purchases
- 5% cash back on gas and groceries (up to $1,500 quarterly)
- 0% intro APR for 15 months on purchases and balance transfers
- Regular APR: 15.99% - 25.99% variable

**Earning Rewards:**
- Earn unlimited cash back
- No category activation required
- Redeem for statement credit, direct deposit, or gift cards
- Rewards never expire

### Travel Rewards Card
Earn points for travel purchases.

**Card Features:**
- Annual fee: $95 (waived first year)
- 3 points per $1 on travel and dining
- 2 points per $1 on all other purchases
- 50,000 bonus points after spending $3,000 in first 3 months
- No foreign transaction fees
- Travel insurance included
- Priority boarding and lounge access

## Application Process

### Online Application
1. Complete application form (10 minutes)
2. Upload required documents
3. Receive instant pre-qualification
4. Full application review (1-2 business days)
5. Final approval and funding

### Credit Decision Factors
- Credit score and history
- Income and employment stability
- Debt-to-income ratio
- Existing relationship with our bank
- Loan purpose and amount
- Collateral value (if applicable)

## Loan Servicing

### Making Payments
- Automatic payment from checking/savings
- Online banking
- Mobile app
- Phone payment
- Mail payment
- In-branch payment

### Payment Due Dates
Payments are due on the same day each month. You can choose your payment date when you set up the loan.

**Grace Period:** 15 days
**Late Payment Fee:** 5% of payment amount or $25 (whichever is greater)

### Early Payoff
All our loans have no prepayment penalties. You can pay off your loan early at any time and save on interest charges.
