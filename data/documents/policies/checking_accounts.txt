# Checking Account Policies

## Account Types

### Free Checking
No monthly fees, perfect for everyday banking.

**Features:**
- No minimum balance requirement
- No monthly maintenance fee
- Free online and mobile banking
- Free debit card
- Free mobile check deposit
- Free bill pay
- Access to 50,000+ fee-free ATMs nationwide
- FDIC insured up to $250,000

**Fees:**
- Overdraft fee: $35 per item (up to 3 per day)
- Non-sufficient funds: $35 per item
- Stop payment: $30
- Foreign ATM fee: $3 per transaction

### Interest Checking
Earn interest on your checking balance.

**Features:**
- Minimum balance: $1,500
- Interest rate: 1.50% APY
- All features of Free Checking
- Free paper checks
- No foreign transaction fees
- Cashback rewards on debit purchases

**Fees:**
- Monthly maintenance fee: $12 (waived with $1,500 minimum balance or $500 monthly direct deposit)

### Premium Checking
Our highest tier checking with exclusive benefits.

**Features:**
- Minimum balance: $10,000
- Interest rate: 2.50% APY
- All ATM fees rebated worldwide
- Free wire transfers (domestic and international)
- Premium credit card with no annual fee
- Identity theft protection
- Travel insurance
- Concierge service

**Fees:**
- Monthly maintenance fee: $25 (waived with $10,000 minimum balance)

## Overdraft Protection

We offer several overdraft protection options:

### Option 1: Link to Savings
Automatically transfer funds from your linked savings account.
- Transfer fee: $10 per transfer
- Transfers in $50 increments

### Option 2: Line of Credit
Apply for an overdraft line of credit.
- Credit line up to $5,000
- Interest rate: Prime + 2%
- No transfer fees

### Option 3: Standard Overdraft Coverage
We may pay overdrafts at our discretion.
- Overdraft fee: $35 per item (maximum 3 per day)
