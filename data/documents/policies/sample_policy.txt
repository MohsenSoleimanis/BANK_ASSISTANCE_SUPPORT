# Bank Policies and Procedures

## Savings Accounts

Our savings accounts offer competitive interest rates with flexible terms. Current rates range from 3.5% to 4.5% APY depending on balance tiers.

### Account Features
- No monthly maintenance fees for balances over $500
- Free online and mobile banking
- FDIC insured up to $250,000
- Unlimited deposits and withdrawals

### Opening Requirements
- Minimum opening deposit: $100
- Valid government-issued ID
- Social Security Number or Tax ID
- Proof of address

## Loan Products

We offer various loan products including personal loans, auto loans, and mortgages.

### Personal Loans
- Amounts from $1,000 to $50,000
- Terms from 12 to 60 months
- Rates starting at 6.99% APR
- Quick approval process

### Eligibility Requirements
- Minimum credit score of 650
- Stable income verification
- Debt-to-income ratio below 40%
- US resident or permanent resident

We take your security seriously and employ industry-leading measures to protect your information.
